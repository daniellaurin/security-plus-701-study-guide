### 🎟️ Discount Tips & Tricks

#### 🎓 Student Discounts
- **Academic Marketplace**: If your school is a CompTIA Academic Partner, you may be eligible for a heavily discounted voucher (~50% off). Ask your IT department or check [CompTIA Academic Marketplace](https://academic.comptia.org/).

#### 🔵 Google Cybersecurity Certificate Discount
Completing the **Google Cybersecurity Certificate on Coursera** makes you eligible for a **30% discount** on a CompTIA Security+ exam voucher through CompTIA's partnership with Google.
- [Complete the Google Cert on Coursera](https://www.coursera.org/google-certificates/cybersecurity-certificate) → then claim your discount via the certificate completion page.


## 💬 Community Advice

>  [**r/CompTIA subreddit**](https://www.reddit.com/r/CompTIA/) and [**r/SecurityPlus**](https://www.reddit.com/r/SecurityPlus/) great place to read exam experience

[![r/CompTIA](https://img.shields.io/badge/Reddit-r%2FCompTIA-FF4500?style=for-the-badge&logo=reddit&logoColor=white)](https://www.reddit.com/r/CompTIA/)
[![r/SecurityPlus](https://img.shields.io/badge/Reddit-r%2FSecurityPlus-FF4500?style=for-the-badge&logo=reddit&logoColor=white)](https://www.reddit.com/r/SecurityPlus/)

**On building genuine interest:**
> *"Not sure if that had stuck if I hadn't been interested in Security long before I started studying. For years I've been watching Blackhat keynotes, reading articles about breaches, getting alerts from NIST, CISA, and Google about ransomware attacks. I play around with basic pen testing tools, have a home lab, taken a ton of Cybrary courses…"*

**On study stack:**
> *"Study bank by Chapple and Seidl, Prof Dion practice quizzes, Quizlet to memorize port numbers, and DojoLabs for simulated labs."*

**On exam-day strategy:**
> *"I skipped the PBQs [performance-based questions] at the beginning of the test, like everyone says to."*  
> *(PBQs appear first but take the longest — flag them and return after answering all multiple choice questions.)*
